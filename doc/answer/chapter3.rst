   (d) We have

   .. math::

      z(t) = x(t)y(t) = \cos(4\pi t)\sin(4\pi t)
         = \frac{1}{2}\sin(8\pi t)\text{.}

   Therefore, the nonzero Fourier series coefficients of :math:`z(t)` are :math:`c_2=c^{*}_{-2}=(1/4j)`.
